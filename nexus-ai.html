The Journey

How NEXUS.AI Works

From a subtle biometric signal to a secured medication delivery — in 3 days.

Day 1
📡
Detection
Nexus detects a 15% dip in activity and sleep patterns through behavioral biometrics, flagging early warning signs of potential non-adherence
Day 2
🔮
Prediction
AI predicts a refill delay due to transport issues using SDoH data. The Friction Model generates real-time risk scores and triggers intervention protocol

Intervention
Guardian Alerts notify the patient's caregiver. System automatically schedules a pharmacy ride, sends personalized nudges, and alerts the clinician
